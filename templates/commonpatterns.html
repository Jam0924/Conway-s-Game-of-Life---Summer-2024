Common Patterns

Glider

This glider will travel down your screen diagonally until it reaches the end. Since this version of Conway's Game of Life is limited, when it reaches the end it becomes a still-life box! Throw another glider at the box to destory it.

Ring of Fire

With this design, it is an oscillating pattern that has the dots around spinning forever in a circle! This is a great pattern for backgrounds and also starting to learn about the possilbities that Conway's Game of Life offers.

Quadpole

Watch these two poles passing back their cells together forever! This is another oscillating pattern that is used frequently as a demonstration for people who are just starting to use Conway's Game of Life! Can you figure out how to make the pole longer?
